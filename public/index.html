Formula 1 Fantasy

Build your dream Formula 1 team! Select two drivers from the current grid and one constructor to compete against other players. Earn points based on Qualifying, overtakes, and finishing positions to win the championship.

How It Works:

Each race weekend, you'll have until the end of Free Practice 1 on Friday to select your lineup. Points are awarded on Saturday for Qualifying performance:

  • Pole Position: 10 pts
  • P2: 9 pts
  • P3: 6 pts
  • P4: 3 pts
  • P5: 1 pt

On Sunday, you'll receive points based on your constructor's performance (highest finishing driver), the number of overtakes your drivers make (2 points per overtake), and your drivers' finishing positions (points awarded as in the actual race).
